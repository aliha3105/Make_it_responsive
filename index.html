What is Inova?

Inova is a consulting firm that specializes in frugal innovation.

We have an office in the heart of Oslo, the capital of Norway. With over 20 employees we continue to grow and reach new heights.

We at Inova think that frugal innvoation is important for Companies and businneses, and we at INOVA want to contribute to help companies to grow and reach their new heights.

What is frugal innovation?

Frugal innovation focuses on the process of reducing the complexity and cost of a product. The pros of doing this is removing the unnecessary parts of a good, like a phone or a product and selling it in developing countries for a cheaper price.
