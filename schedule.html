Innovation camp

DATE 24.01.2020 01.02.2020 20.03.2020 07.06.2020 12.08.2020
The camp is hosted at Oslo Gardermoen airport. With young talents between the ages of 16-19 years old from across the world. This day will be used to introduce the participants to each other, and to the endless opportunities that exist in the computer science field. This day will be dedicated to taking a deeper dive into what is innovation. Many have the misconception that to be innovative you must make a ground-breaking, never thought of solution. The participants will get to learn some innovation methods and strategies. Time will also be set aside to work on the case project The field of computer science is not only about coding and making some cool websites. Computer science can be ground-breaking, innovative and can even help solve social challenges. That is what will speak about. After lunch there will be round table talk with different software engineers, owners of start-ups discussing Its growing role Since the end of the week is approaching. This whole day will be dedicated to working on the case project. The schedule above is self-explanatory. At the end of Friday our winners, runner ups and second runner ups will be announced.
